On average, Princess Cruises has 1.7 million passengers and 30,000 employees sailing around the world every year. The organization needed an integrated global communications system that connected all of their assets. Now with IBM Cloud, messaging, calendar and IM services have transformed 18 ships — spread across five oceans — into a single fleet.Sudden weather delays and coordinating the movement of thousands of passengers are now facilitated in real time with social, which can help transform the customer experience and ultimately increase loyalty.

Toshiba, a multinational engineering and electronics conglomerate, is working with IBM's R&D to transform its business model. From a manufacturer of electronics, using cloud and data, they have evolved into a provider of added value to their products.By employing usage data in the cloud to reduce wasteful processor idling, decrease energy consumption, lower emissions, and extend the longevity of the devices, Toshiba can continue to work to improve the performance of their products even after they have been sold. Another example of how data is making the planet smarter.

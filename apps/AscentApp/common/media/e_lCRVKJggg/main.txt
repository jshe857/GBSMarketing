With more than 1.5 million square feet, over 24,000 parking spaces and 75,000 seats, Sun Life Stadium has countless moving parts. In the past, it relied on complex systems to manage everything from parking to concession sales and weather data. But now, after integrating their systems on IBM Cloud, Sun Life Stadium has a consolidated view of their operations. Thanks to improved efficiency, along with the ability to connect with audience memebers via mobile phone, Sun Life Stadium can make everyone at the game feel like a winner.

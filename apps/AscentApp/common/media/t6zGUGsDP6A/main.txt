For Lindt, one of the oldest and most popular confectioners in the world, scale came along with a few challenges. Customers who bought chocolates online had a limited choice in the form of prepackaged assortments.Now on IBM Cloud, Lindt’s e-commerce platform is putting customers back in control, enabling them to create unique assortments, mix and match their favorite products, and design custom packaging based on their individual tastes. Satisfied customers are leading to greater loyalty and better sales.
